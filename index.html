Project Share (Android & IOS App)

As a first generation college student, I know how hard it can be to find beginner CS opportunities outside of school. I came up with an app where students can explore and find CS opportunities they may be interested in such as open source, mobile and web development etc. With colaboration from 2 other students, we were able to build an app that can...

  • Register and Login users (users can be students, mentors, industry etc).
  • Users can see a list of projects with a description
  • Users can post and comment on projects

My goal for Project Share is to get students involved with CS early in their college career.
